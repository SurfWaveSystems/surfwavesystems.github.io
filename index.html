Technology

SurfWave introduces a new class of antennas powered by an intelligent computation layer that shapes the transmitted wave itself. Instead of relying on large antenna arrays and complex radio systems, our thin programmable layer gently reshapes the outgoing signal so it naturally focuses toward users more efficiently.

This principle mirrors how LCDs replaced bulky CRTs — using a thin, intelligent surface to control wave propagation more efficiently. Our approach delivers strong, stable coverage with significantly lower power consumption and far less hardware.

Performance

Based on system-level simulations and early prototyping, our architecture demonstrates:

  • 4× higher throughput
  • 10× better energy efficiency
  • 4 orders of magnitude lower symbol error rate

We have a first prototype validating our analysis methodology and a letter of interest from a leading North American telecom service provider.

Impact

Higher communication gain enables better indoor coverage, fewer access points, and larger cell sizes — reducing interference, installation complexity, and maintenance overhead. This translates into lower CAPEX, lower OPEX, faster deployments, and stronger economics for partners.
